Training Log

Your offline training companion. Build a week, train, log every set. Everything stays on your phone, no account, no internet needed.

  • Build a week: pick your goal, days, time and kit. It builds instantly, fully offline.
  • Log as you go: set your reps, tap each set done, nudge the weight up or down.
  • Technique: open Form notes on any exercise for the coaching cues.

Tip: in Safari, tap Share then “Add to Home Screen” to keep it on your phone.
